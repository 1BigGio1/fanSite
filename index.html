Main Characters In The Game

Micheal De Santa

Boss

Michael's ability is called Bullet Time. When active Michael enters a bullet time like effect allowing him to slow down time and gain the advantage in shootouts. This ability lasts about 30 seconds. To enhance this ability you have to do: Headshots: 7.5% bonus, Stealth kill or knock out: 10% bonus, Health drop below 25%: 20% bonus and Maintainig high speeds in vehicles: small bonus.

Trevor Phillips

Gunner

Trevor's special ability is Red Mist, which allows him to take less damage and inflict more damge on others, practically making him invincible. increasing his ablility you have to make him angry. To enhance his ablility you have to: Failing a mission: small bonus, Fall over: small bonus, Take damage: small bonus, Rammed by veichle: small bonus, Bumping into pedestrian: small bonus, Headshots: 7.5% bonus, Kill at least one pedestrian: small bonus and Maintaining high speeds in vehicles: small bonus.

Franklin Clinton

Driver

Franklin has a special ability called Driving Focus which allows him to slow down time while driving any land vehicle. This allows him to easily take corners at full speed and make precise maneuvers quickly. He can also enhance this ablility by: Drifting around corners adds a continuous bonus, Near vehicle misses at high speed: small bonus, Driving on the wrong side of the street in traffic adds a continuous bonus and Driving at or above 90% of vehicle's top speed: continuous bonus.
